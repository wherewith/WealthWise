About us

WealthWise is designed to be an all-in-one solution to financial education for people of all ages. Through useful tools for users as well as an extensive wiki page on financial topics, we seek to solve the financial literacy gap between the lower and upper class and improve the standard of living for any american who feels unconfident in their finances.


WealthWise was designed during TAMUhack 2023 by Kunal Jain and Rahul Rajendran.
